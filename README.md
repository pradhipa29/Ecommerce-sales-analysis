# E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 100,000+ orders from Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset
using SQL to find revenue trends, top products, delivery performance
and customer behavior across 8 tables.

## Tools Used
- SQLite
- DB Browser for SQLite

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: $16,008,872
- Peak Month: November 2017 — $1.19M (Black Friday)
- Top State: São Paulo — 37% of total revenue
- Top Category: Health & Beauty — $1.25M
- Top Payment: Credit Card — 78% of revenue
- Delivery Success Rate: 97%
- Fastest Delivery: São Paulo — 8.8 days average
- Repeat Purchase Rate: Only 3.12%

## Queries Covered
1. Total Revenue
2. Monthly Revenue Trend
3. Top 10 States by Revenue
4. Top 10 Product Categories
5. Order Status Breakdown
6. Payment Type Analysis
7. Top 10 Sellers by Revenue
8. Review Score by Category
9. Delivery Days by State
10. Customer Repeat Purchase Rate

## Dataset
- Source: Olist Brazilian E-commerce
- Platform: Kaggle
- Size: 100,000+ orders · 8 tables
